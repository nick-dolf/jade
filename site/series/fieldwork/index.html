Billow [Performance - Special Projects]
Handmade costumes, sewn fabric, and helium balloons
Performed with Sara Jimenez

Fieldwork surveys the performativity of queerness through visual donning of elaborate costumes and sculptures through abstract movement. In particular, the work looks at post-performance residue, of what is left after a moment of showmanship and spectacle.

2014
